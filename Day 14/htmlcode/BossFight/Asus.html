About Us

ASUS is a Taiwan-based, multinational computer hardware and consumer electronics company that was established in 1989. Dedicated to creating products for today's and tomorrow's smart life, ASUS is the world's No. 1 motherboard and gaming brand as well as a top-three consumer notebook vendor. ASUS today announced that it has been included in TIME magazine's list of the World's Best Companies for 2023.
